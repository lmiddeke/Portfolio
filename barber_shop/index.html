Founded in 1956
We have a long-standing history of making men feel their best

Masimo Mercurio founded Barberossa in 1956 with his kid brother Mario. Together they started cutting hair from their front porch, to eventually owning their own store.

This in turn became the most well-known shop on the street.

Barberossa barbers are all highly experienced in their craft.
They always leave you looking good.
